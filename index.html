This is my story...

Hi my name is Tobiáš. I am student of Faculty of Information Technology in BUT. I was born on 5. february 2002 at hospital in Trstená. I live in Nižná which is small village on the north of Slovakia. I have one older brother David and happily married parents. I went to high school to Gymnázium of Martin Hattala in Trstená. After this decision my life changed for 180 degrees. I had to travell to school everyday. I needed to become more adult than before. I also started playing on violin and I had a lot of after-school activities. I am very active and creative person so I have to spent my energy somewhere. I also joined theathre scene on Orava Castle and started doing things I love.

HOBBIES

CODING

I started coding on my high school. We started in program called Imagine where you need to write code for little green turtle. I very enjoyed that class since we started programming. I was probably the best in my class and I often spend all of my time on PC to program in Imagine. Later we swapped to C++ in CodeBlocks and after that I started to think about programming career. I also choose BUT mainly because I want to be good at programming and this school is really helpful right now.

ACTING

My next and probably the best hobbie I am enjoying at the moment. Since I was a little kid I've always wanted to play on TV. I started in some amateur shows in my village but since 2019 I am part of actors of Oravské Múzeum and also part of dNO which stands for divadlo Námestovo. I already did some project such as opera Jesus Christ Superstar, Jewish Show and many more theatricals on Orava Castle. I really enjoy being part of this community and I like make people laugh because one smile can change many lives.

FLOORBALL

One of my first hobbies was floorball. I mean everybody in my hometown play this sport. Everybody living for this sport here and everybody want to be good at it. When I was 6 years old I got floorball stick on Christmas and after that I signed to play floorball for our school. I was really good and maybe the best one since my doctor said that I have an astma. I really did broke down but after that I joined one of the best floorball clubs on Slovakia : FBK Nižná and I started to work on myself again. I really enjoy playing floorball, more than any other sports and I wanna become helpful member for my team and win a trophy for them.
